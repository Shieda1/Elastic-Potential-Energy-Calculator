What is the elastic potential energy?
Elastic potential energy is the energy that is sorted as a result of applying a force to deform an elastic object.
How to calculate elastic potential energy?
To calculate elastic potential energy you should use the following formula:
U = 1/2 * k * ΔX^2
where:
U = elastic energy
K = spring constant
ΔX = change in position
Why use elastic potential energy?
The elastic potential energy calculator is used in order to measure the applied force that is used to deform an object.
